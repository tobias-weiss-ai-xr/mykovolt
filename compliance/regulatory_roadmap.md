# Regulatory Compliance Framework

## Executive Summary

This document outlines the comprehensive regulatory compliance framework for the MykoVolt bio-battery project. The framework addresses all regulatory requirements related to EU Battery Regulation, environmental standards, safety protocols, and quality assurance. This framework ensures compliance with local, national, European, and international regulations throughout the product lifecycle.

## Regulatory Overview

### Primary Regulatory Frameworks

#### 1. EU Battery Regulation (EU 2023/1069)
- **Scope**: All batteries placed on the EU market
- **Effective Date**: August 2024
- **Key Requirements**:
  - Labeling requirements
  - Performance standards
  - Recycling targets
  - Safety requirements

#### 2. REACH Regulation (EC 1907/2006)
- **Scope**: Chemical substances and mixtures
- **Effective Date**: Existing regulations
- **Key Requirements**:
  - Substance registration
  - Risk assessment
  - Restriction of hazardous substances

#### 3. WEEE Directive (EU 2020/1028)
- 3. **Scope**: Waste electrical and electronic equipment
- **Effective Date**: Existing regulations
- **Key Requirements**:
  - Collection targets
  - Recycling targets
  - Producer responsibility

#### 4. RoHS Directive (EU 2011/65/EU)
- **Scope**: Restriction of hazardous substances
- **Effective Date**: Existing regulations
- **Key Requirements**:
  - Maximum concentration limits
  - Product restrictions
  - Documentation requirements

#### 5. CLP Regulation (EC 1272/2008)
- **Scope**: Classification, labeling, and packaging of substances and mixtures
- **Effective Date**: Existing regulations
- **Key Requirements**:
  - Hazard classification
  - Label requirements
  - Safety data sheets

## Product Compliance Requirements

### 1. Chemical Compliance

#### Substance Compliance
- **Restricted Substances**:
  - Lead (Pb): <0.1% weight
  - Mercury (Hg): <0.01% weight
  - Cadmium (Cd): <0.01% weight
  - Polybrominated biphenyls (PBBs): <0.1% weight
  - Polybrominated diphenyl ethers (PBDEs): <0.1% weight

- **Safety Data Sheets**: Complete SDS for all materials
